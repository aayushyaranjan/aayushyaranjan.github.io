To August

Aayushya Ranjan

To August,
With you the waining leaves always meant the rising pain.Â 
The absurd loneliness seemed to envelope the miniscule emerging happiness.
Be gentle now.
My legs are tired.
I look at the skies of the leaving monsoon,
And no echo.
I've sat now.
I'm tired of walking.
I've been meaning to lay down,
But I am also scared of sleeping.
Be gentle now.
Just this once, for my sake August,
Be gentle for my sake.
